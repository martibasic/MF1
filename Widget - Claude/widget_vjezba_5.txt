Evo detaljnih promptova za Gemini za svaki zadatak iz Vježbe 5 – Mehanika fluida:

Zadatak 37 – Trokut s gornjim kutom na površini
Prompt:

Create an interactive HTML/JS/CSS widget that visualizes hydrostatic pressure on a triangle submerged in fluid with its apex touching the surface. The triangle should be drawn vertically with the tip at the water surface and the base at the bottom. Animate a pressure distribution diagram (triangular/trapezoidal shape) on the left side of the triangle that grows as you increase fluid density using a slider. Draw and animate the centroid T (center of mass, at 2h/3 from top) and the center of pressure Ch (at 3h/4 from top) as labeled colored dots. Show a vertical arrow representing the resultant hydrostatic force F_h whose length scales with density. Add a second slider for triangle height h. Display the formula z_Fh = 3h/4 and update all values numerically in real time. The pressure field should be visualized as horizontal blue gradient lines that get denser and darker with depth. Add a legend explaining T (centroid) and Ch (center of pressure). Use smooth animations. Label axes z (depth) pointing downward.


Zadatak 38 – Trokut uronjen na dubini a
Prompt:

Create an interactive HTML/JS/CSS widget for hydrostatic force on a triangle submerged at depth 'a' below the free surface (apex down, base up, or apex up at depth a). Include three sliders: triangle height h, base b, and submersion depth a. Draw the water surface at the top, the triangle below it starting at depth a. Visualize the pressure distribution as a trapezoid on the triangle face (since neither edge is at zero pressure). Animate the centroid T and center of pressure Ch as labeled dots with vertical positions updating in real time. Show the resultant force arrow scaling with computed F_h. Display the formula z_Fh = (6a² + 8ah + 3h²) / (6(a + 2h/3)) and update it numerically. Color the pressure gradient from light blue at depth a to dark blue at depth a+h. Add an info panel showing z_T, z_Fh, I_T, A values. Animate smoothly when sliders change.

Zadatak 40 – Polukrug s tetivom na površini

Zadatak 41 – Pravokutna zaklopka ovješena u točki A

The tank is filled with water to height h2 from the bottom, the gate has height h1. Add sliders for h1 (gate height, 1–5 m), h2 (water level, 1–6 m), and width b. Draw the tank cross-section with the gate on the right wall. Animate the hydrostatic pressure distribution as a trapezoid on the gate (zero at water surface, max at bottom). Show the resultant force arrow F_h pointing left, with its point of application Ch. Animate the hinge reaction at A and sill reaction at B as force arrows that update when sliders change. Display F_h = ρ·g·z_T·A and z_Fh formulas with live numerical values. Color water blue with transparency. When h2 < h2_top_of_gate, show partial submersion. Add a moment equilibrium panel showing M_A balance. Make everything physically accurate and animated.


Zadatak 42 – Nakošena zaklopka s kutom 45°
Prompt:

Create an interactive HTML/JS/CSS widget for an inclined rectangular gate at angle α to the horizontal, hinged at B (top), resting on the tank bottom at A, with water on one side. Add sliders for: angle α (15°–75°), water depth h1 above the gate top, water depth h2 (gate portion submerged), width b, and gate mass m. Draw the inclined gate as a rectangle tilted at α. Show the inclined coordinate system zi along the gate and z vertical. Visualize hydrostatic pressure as lines perpendicular to the gate surface, increasing in magnitude toward A. Show the resultant force F_R arrow normal to the gate at Ch. Show the applied opening force F (perpendicular to gate at centroid) as a separate arrow. Display the moment balance about B: F·(L/2) = F_R·(z_Fhi - z_Bi). Animate: as you change sliders, the force arrows scale, Ch moves, and the minimum F required updates in real time. Show the inclined axis zi with z_Ti and z_Fhi marked. Use the formula z_Fhi = z_Ti + I_x0/(z_Ti·A).
