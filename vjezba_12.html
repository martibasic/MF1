12.1 Mehanika fluida – Vježba 12

12.1.1 Zadatak 104: Potisak satelita

Rješenje: 1. \(F_p = \dot{m} v_i = 50 \cdot 3000 = \mathbf{150 \, \text{kN}}\). 2. \(a_s = F_p / m_s = \mathbf{30 \, \text{m/s}^2}\). 3. \(\Delta v = a_s \Delta t = \mathbf{60 \, \text{m/s}}\).

12.1.2 Zadatak 105: Sila na prirubnici slavine

Rješenje: 1. \(v_1 = v_2 = 3,724 \, \text{m/s}\). 2. \(F_{Rx} = -p_1 A_1 - \beta \dot{m} v_1 = \mathbf{-32,5 \, \text{N}}\). 3. \(F_{Rz} = G - \beta \dot{m} v_2 = \mathbf{52,55 \, \text{N}}\).

12.1.3 Zadatak 107: Snaga rotirajuće prskalice

Rješenje: 1. \(v_{rel} = 63,66 \, \text{m/s}, v_{oboda} = 18,85 \, \text{m/s} \implies v_{aps} = 44,81 \, \text{m/s}\). 2. \(M = \dot{m} v_{aps} r = \mathbf{537,7 \, \text{Nm}}\). 3. \(P = M \omega = \mathbf{16,9 \, \text{kW}}\).

12.1.4 Zadatak 108: Komercijalne vjetroturbine

Tekst zadatka: Vjetroturbina promjera \(90 \, \text{m}\) generira snagu pri brzini vjetra \(25 \, \text{km/h}\) uz učinkovitost \(32\%\). Odrediti snagu i silu na stup.

Rješenje: 1. \(v_1 = 6,94 \, \text{m/s}, \dot{m} = 55223 \, \text{kg/s}\). 2. \(P_{max} = 1331 \, \text{kW} \implies P = \mathbf{426 \, \text{kW}}\). 3. \(v_2 = v_1 \sqrt{1-0,32} = 5,73 \, \text{m/s}\). 4. \(F_R = \dot{m}(v_2 - v_1) = \mathbf{-67,2 \, \text{kN}}\).

12.1.5 Zadatak 109: Snaga helikoptera

Rješenje: 1. Prazan: \(v = 21,7 \, \text{m/s} \implies P = \mathbf{1064 \, \text{kW}}\). 2. S teretom: \(v = 34,3 \, \text{m/s} \implies P = \mathbf{4205 \, \text{kW}}\).

12.1.6 Zadatak 111: Peltonova turbina

Tekst zadatka: Dokazati izraz za snagu Peltonove turbine: \(P = \rho \dot{V} (v-w) w (1 - \cos\beta)\). Odrediti snagu za \(v=50 \, \text{m/s}, \dot{V}=150 \, \text{L/s}, r=2 \, \text{m}, n=150 \, \text{o/min}, \beta=160^\circ\).

Rješenje: 1. \(w = r \omega = 31,42 \, \text{m/s}\). 2. \(P = 1000 \cdot 0,15 \cdot (50-31,42) \cdot 31,42 \cdot (1 - \cos 160^\circ) = \mathbf{11,3 \, \text{MW}}\).

12.1.7 Zadatak 112: Flyboard dinamika

Rješenje: 1. Za lebdenje (\(150 \, \text{kg}\)): \(v_{min} = \mathbf{13,69 \, \text{m/s}}\). 2. Pri \(15 \, \text{m/s}\): \(a = \mathbf{1,97 \, \text{m/s}^2}\).

Dinamika potiska (Flyboard)

Ubrzava prema gore!
